About me

Jan Poláček

Embarking on my creative journey in late 2017, I delved into the realm of music production, crafting soulful lo-fi tunes on my computer, showcased on Soundcloud. This venture sparked my foray into graphic design as I took on the role of a self-designer, curating visually captivating Soundcloud designs and album covers for my evolving discography.

By 2018, my creations found a broader audience on platforms like Soundcloud (now using Over Yoghurt) and Newgrounds, gaining recognition when my music was featured in the 2022 Geometry Dash level, 'Just Dance,' by GrimScythe. Since 2019, I've been meticulously building my brand identity and garnering recognition within the creative sphere. The onset of the Covid-19 pandemic in 2020 prompted me to extend my reach, collaborating with amateur esports leagues, providing graphic design services for teams such as Frontis, Quil, LowPulse, and more.

Throughout my high school studies, I honed my skills further by crafting and self-designing small websites for clients like OGORacing and autodeformace.cz, cultivating a modest yet loyal following in the region. Since 2021, I've proudly served as the main designer for the non-profit organization EFRL contributing graphic designs for their Instagram, Facebook, and various other platforms, catering to both players and their diverse clientele.

As I continue this creative journey, I am eager to explore new opportunities and contribute my skills to diverse projects, seeking a meaningful job opportunity that allows me to gain valuable experiences in the regions I am passionate about.

If interested, you can send me a mail or follow me up on LinkedIn.
