About Me

Image: Photo of Jan Poláček

Hello there,

I call myself Neostetic, full name Jan "Honzik" Poláček. I’m a multimedia creator focused on music, graphic design, and programming, building on reliability, bold decisions, and handcrafted detail.

Since 2017, I’ve been shaping distinctive work that blends creativity with technology. I’m always open to new ideas, collaborations, and projects that push boundaries — let’s create something bold together.

PNKSTR

PNKSTR is a brand through which I’ve been reviving my music journey. It blends multiple forms of media — from t-shirt design to punk-inspired and gothic aesthetics — into one cohesive visual identity.

Music is at the core of everything I do. It’s raw, expressive, and constantly evolving. As the project grows, I’ll continue sharing its progress and direction.

Drive Safe, Caution!

Drive Safe, Caution!, also known as DSC, is a design project inspired by car culture. It captures the feeling of driving on the edge while still staying in control and keeping a safe distance.

Experience the thrill — even when driving safe.

reTHAWed – Online Banner

In 2025, I had the opportunity to work on a mod for one of my beloved childhood games — reTHAWed (mod for Tony Hawk's: American Wasteland).

I created multiple posters featured in the main lobby, where players could experience my work firsthand. I’m looking forward to collaborating with this amazing team again.

KEEP YOUR STANDARDS HIGH

The poster KEEP YOUR STANDARDS HIGH reflects our inner state — the pressure we put on ourselves and the constant voice in our heads. It’s a reminder to slow down from time to time and find balance.

Designed with a punk-inspired aesthetic, it uses multiple blending techniques to create seamless transitions between elements, forming one cohesive visual piece.

MD2F

Markdown2File (or MD2F) is a framework I developed to create a fast and reliable way of working with Markdown for my personal projects. It was built to streamline content processing and improve overall workflow efficiency.

I continuously maintain and improve the framework, exploring new ways to process Markdown faster and more effectively.

#fullstack-development
#website-development
#framework
Image: MD2F website

PixelbiteCSS

PixelbiteCSS is a framework designed to simplify web development. It leverages a system of variables and utility classes to shift CSS toward a class-based approach, reducing the need for traditional styling.

It’s flexible, scalable, and built for creating reusable components across entire projects.

#frontend-development
#framework
#UI-UX
Image: PixelbiteCSS's website

EFRL – Graphic Design

I worked on developing the visual language for EFRL across three seasons, starting from Season 3. I was responsible for creating the overall branding, incorporating sponsor assets and existing logos into a cohesive design system.

The goal was to maintain a consistent and recognizable identity throughout each season while adapting to evolving content and partnerships.
