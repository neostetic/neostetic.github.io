PNKSTR

PNKSTR is a personal brand project developed as a multidisciplinary platform connecting my work across graphic design, music, and fashion. It was created with the intention of building a cohesive identity that allows these mediums to coexist and evolve within a unified creative direction.

Challenge

The primary challenge was to establish a visual language that feels authentic, expressive, and adaptable across various outputs. The identity needed to function consistently across digital content, merchandise, and music-related visuals while maintaining a strong and recognizable character.

Visual Direction

The visual identity is built around bold typography, high contrast elements, and a mix of structured and chaotic layouts. Typography acts as both a functional and expressive element, while the overall system remains flexible enough to allow variation without losing consistency.

Execution

The project spans multiple applications including posters, digital visuals, social media outputs, and merchandise concepts. Each output explores different aspects of the identity while reinforcing the overall tone and direction of the brand.
